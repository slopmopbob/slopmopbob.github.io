Spawn & States

A configurable state engine for generating entities with layered traits, dynamic variables, and emergent compound states.

Define attributes, traits, variables, modifiers, and compounds in a single JSON config. Connect them with typed relationships. The engine handles weighted trait selection, variable ticking, threshold triggers, and compound emergence automatically.

Entities spawn from presets or dynamic generation, live in managed pools, and evolve through a configurable tick system. One config produces thousands of unique entities with distinct behaviors. Available as a JavaScript library with visual editor and a Godot 4.x plugin.

Mabel's Tale

In Development

A 2D pixel art narrative adventure where you run a medieval tavern that doubles as a halfway house for cult escapees. Mabel listens, serves drinks, and slowly pieces together what happened to the people who walk through her door.

Spawn & States powers the personality system. 23 personality types across 6 emotional spectrums generate dynamic moods that drive contextual dialogue. Same engine, different scale: from abstract config to living characters.

About

I'm an independent game developer focused on the systems layer: the part where you define rules and watch interesting things happen that you didn't explicitly author. Procedural generation, emergent behavior, simulation.

Spawn & States started as a patron generator for a tavern sim and grew into a general-purpose state engine. I'm building it as both a standalone tool and the backbone of Mabel's Tale.
